About Eli

Eli, short for Elizabeth, has sold over 1000 original oil paintings in the last 20 years. She also licenses her images with companies to make prints and housewares. She grew up in Alaska, but now lives in Austin Texas.

Original Oil Paintings

Eli paints on wood and canvas of a wide variety of sizes. She is happy to walk you through the simple process of purchasing and shipping to get you on your way to investing in happy walls! Payment plans available.
